# Data Retention Quick Reference

If you just need the numbers without reading the full policy document:

- Financial records are retained for 7 years, primarily to support tax and audit compliance.
- Employee records are retained for 3 years after termination, primarily to support employment-dispute liability windows.
- Customer support tickets are retained for 2 years, primarily to support product-quality trend analysis.

See the full Data Retention Policy document for the formal deletion
process. This note is a personal quick-reference, not the source of
truth.
